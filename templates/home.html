ABOUT JOVIANS

It’s hard to move on and let go. But it’s your mindset that can be your greatest asset or worst enemy. One ending is not your end. You can use a divorce or separation as a chance to work on yourself and transform. Pain can be a great catalyst. That’s why it’s great to have someone on your team who can aid you. And I’m that person who can help you get there. I’m a breakup recovery/attachment coach who helps clients heal, let go, and move on from old individuals and patterns, so that they experience a wholesome, healthy self-love and marriage. My sessions are affordable, transformative, and life-changing. We begin by analyzing your past and prior choices to uncover your limiting beliefs and stored associations. Then, we work together on healing old wounds and setting in action plans through a myriad of exercises to help you overcome these challenges. Last, we ensure that you implement the plan and track your progress. I’m with you every step of the way. Having a coach is the best way to learn new strategies, stay motivated, and keep yourself accountable. You don’t need to be a lone wolf. You can finally get the support and encouragement you deserve.
